Chocolate Pudding

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 2 3/4 cups whole milk
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. In a medium saucepan, whisk together the granulated sugar, cocoa powder, cornstarch, and salt.
  2. Gradually whisk in the whole milk until smooth.
  3. Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook, whisking constantly, until the mixture thickens and comes to a boil, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Once boiling, continue to cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly.
  5. In a small bowl, lightly beat the egg yolks. Gradually whisk in about 1 cup of the hot cocoa mixture to temper the eggs.
  6. Pour the tempered egg mixture back into the saucepan with the remaining cocoa mixture, whisking constantly.
  7. Cook for another 2 minutes, stirring constantly, until the pudding is thickened.
  8. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the unsalted butter and vanilla extract until the butter is melted and the mixture is smooth.
  9. Transfer the pudding to serving dishes or a large bowl and cover the surface with plastic wrap to prevent a skin from forming.
  10. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or until the pudding is set and completely chilled.
  11. Serve chilled, optionally with whipped cream or chocolate shavings on top.
