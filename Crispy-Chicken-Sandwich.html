Crispy Chicken Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Mayonnaise
  • Pickles (optional)

Steps:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. In a shallow dish, combine the all-purpose flour, paprika, garlic powder, and cayenne pepper (if using).
  3. Dip each chicken breast into the flour mixture, shaking off any excess.
  4. Dip the chicken breasts into the beaten eggs, then coat them with breadcrumbs.
  5. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Fry the chicken breasts for about 5-6 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Remove the chicken from the skillet and drain on paper towels.
  7. Toast the hamburger buns on the skillet or in a toaster until lightly golden.
  8. Assemble the sandwiches by placing a fried chicken breast on the bottom half of each bun.
  9. Top with lettuce leaves, sliced tomatoes, mayonnaise, and pickles (if using).
  10. Cover with the top half of the bun and serve immediately.
