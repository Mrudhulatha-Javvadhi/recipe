Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Store-bought pizza dough or homemade pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, etc.)
  • Olive oil for brushing (optional)
  • Italian seasoning (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to the temperature specified on the pizza dough package or recipe.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, then add your desired toppings.
  6. If desired, brush the edges of the crust with olive oil and sprinkle with Italian seasoning.
  7. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven according to the dough package or recipe instructions, typically 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  8. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
