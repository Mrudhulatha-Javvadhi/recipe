Chocolate chip cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together flour and baking soda. Set aside.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  4. Beat in eggs, one at a time, until well incorporated.
  5. Gradually blend dry ingredients into creamed mixture.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Drop rounded spoonfuls of dough onto prepared baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes, or until edges are golden brown.
  9. Cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
  10. Enjoy your delicious chocolate chip cookies!
