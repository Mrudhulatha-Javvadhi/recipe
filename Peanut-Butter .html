Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup peanut butter (creamy or crunchy)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream together the peanut butter and granulated sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract until well combined.
  4. Roll the dough into 1-inch balls and place them on the prepared baking sheets.
  5. Use a fork to flatten each ball of dough and create a crisscross pattern on top.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 to 12 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.
  7. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely.
