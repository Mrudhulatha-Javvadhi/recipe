Cheeseburger

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup and mustard (optional)

    • Steps:

    1. Divide the ground beef into four equal portions and shape them into patties. Season both sides of each patty with salt and pepper.
    2. Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
    3. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty and cover the skillet or grill to melt the cheese.
    4. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster until lightly golden.
    5. Assemble the burgers by placing a cooked patty with melted cheese on the bottom half of each bun.
    6. Top with lettuce leaves, sliced tomatoes, onions, pickles (if using), and condiments like ketchup and mustard.
    7. Cover with the top half of the bun and serve immediately.
